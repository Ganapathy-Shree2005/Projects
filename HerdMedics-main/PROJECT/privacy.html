Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2025

Data Collection

HerdMedics values your privacy and ensures that data collection is limited to the information necessary for delivering our services. We collect personal data such as your name, email address, and contact details, along with cattle health-related data, images, and metadata used for disease detection. Your data is securely stored and never shared without explicit consent.

Use of Information

All collected information is utilized to improve system accuracy, personalize user experience, and enhance disease prediction models. We also use this data for customer support, internal analytics, and research to further veterinary advancements.

Security and Protection

Ensuring the confidentiality and security of your data is our top priority. We implement state-of-the-art encryption protocols, secured cloud storage, and access control measures to prevent unauthorized access or data breaches.

Third-Party Integrations

HerdMedics may collaborate with veterinary professionals, research institutions, and health organizations to enhance disease detection efficiency. However, any third-party data exchange is anonymized and strictly regulated to maintain compliance with global privacy standards.

Legal Compliance

We adhere to international privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Users have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of their personal data at any time by contacting our support team.

Device Access and Tracking

For real-time disease analysis, our platform may request access to your camera and GPS location. This access is granted only with user approval and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

Policy Updates

We continuously refine our privacy policies to reflect technological and regulatory advancements. Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please reach out to us at support@herdmedics.com.
